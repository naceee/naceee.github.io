Cycling baby boom

Have you ever wondered what kind of cyclist would be born if Tadej Pogačar and Primož Roglič had a child together? Well... They are both guys, but what if anyway? Here is a silly project exploring such questions. You can enter two male cyclists (that were active in world tour in 2024) and make a baby. The resulting baby will be an active cyclist with his skill set similar to the average skill set of the two selected cyclists. To make it more fun there is a little bit of randomness involved, so you can try a few times and get even more babies.
